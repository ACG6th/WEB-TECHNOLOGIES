Churros


Ingredients

  • 1 cup water (240ml)
  • 2 tablespoon unsalted butter (28g)
  • 2 tablespoon sugar (13g)
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (120g)
  • 1 large egg (50g))
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Cinnamon Sugar

  • 1/2 cup sugar (100g)
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Chocolate Dipping Saucer

  • 3 oz bittersweet chocolate (85g)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (120g)

Preparation

  1. Add the ingredients together and stir; press it and let it cool for 5 minutes.
  2. Add one egg and teaspoon of vanilla extract and mix.
  3. Use a piping bag to create churros.
  4. Fry the churros (350F), and fry for 5 minutes on each side.
  5. Drain on paper towel.
  6. Create the cinammon sugar to coat the churros.
  7. Create the chocolate dipping sauce.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
