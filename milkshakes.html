Milkshakes


Ingredients

  • 1 cup (150g) of any ice-cream flavour
  • 1/3 cup (80ml) Milk
  • Whipped cream for topping (optional)

Preparation

  1. To the blender, add the milk, then the rest of the ingredients..
  2. Blend for 1-2 cycles.
  3. Pour into a glass, top with whipped cream and the topping (optional)..
  4. To make whipped cream: in a bowl whip heavy cream with powdered sugar. Whip to stiff peaks, then transfer to a piping bag.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
