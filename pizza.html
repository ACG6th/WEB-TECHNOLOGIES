Hawaiian Pizza


Ingredients

  • Onions, sliced
  • Garlic, sliced
  • 1/2 tbsp of Olive oil
  • 1/8 kilo of Ground pork
  • 1/2 tsp of Black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp of Seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp of Salt to taste
  • 1 pack Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • 1 pack pineapple tidbits
  • ham, cut into square
  • Sausage
  • Grated Parmesan

Preparation

  1. Put some olive oil in the cooking pan, add onion, garlic, black pepper, seasoning, ground pork, salt and pizza sauce.
  2. Put the cooked pork ground into separate container.
  3. Cooked ground pork, ham, sausage, seasoning, mozzarella cheese, parmesan cheese and pineapple tidbits ..
  4. Mix together.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
